About

Background

I'm a neurology resident at the University of Texas Medical Branch (UTMB). I completed my Medical Doctorate at Seoul National University and my Masters of Public Health at Harvard University. My clinical interests span cognitive neurology, epilepsy, movement disorders, neuroimmunology, and neuro-oncology. My research focuses on dementia, synaptic neurophysiology, and drug development.

I'm captivated by where science and technology meet real-world application — bench to bedside. I pursue breakthroughs in neuroscience and neurology not just to understand them, but to leverage them: solving meaningful problems, building around them, and making their impact felt in the world.

This site is where I make sense of what I'm learning. The writing here isn't polished commentary — it's honest thinking-in-progress.
